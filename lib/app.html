What makes a codebase truly maintainable long-term?
⌥
Maintainability isn't a feature you add — it's a discipline you practice. Three things matter most:

Readability over cleverness. Code is read 10× more than it's written. Optimize for the reader, not the writer. Name things what they are.

Small, honest functions. A function that does one thing and says so in its name is worth a thousand comments. If you need to describe what it does with "and", split it.

Delete aggressively. Dead code is the enemy. It creates cognitive load, misleads future readers, and rots. The best line of code is the one you didn't write.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know before you launch.

Do I need to create an account?▾
Yes — a free account is required to use Apex. Registration takes about 30 seconds and only needs your name, email, and a password. There's no credit card, no subscription, and no trial period. Your account lets us securely save your conversations, memories, and settings across all your devices.
Is my data private?▾
Yes. Your conversations are stored securely on our server, protected by encrypted JWT authentication. We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models. When you send a message it goes from your browser → our server (authenticated) → AI provider. Your passwords are bcrypt-hashed and never stored in plain text.
How much does it cost?▾
Apex is completely free to use. We cover the AI API costs so you don't have to bring your own API keys. Just create a free account and start using it immediately — no payment information required.
What is Ultra mode?▾
Ultra mode fires your message at all three AI engines simultaneously — Claude Opus 4, GPT-4o, and Gemini 1.5 Pro — and shows you the response from whichever finishes first. You get the fastest answer from the best available model, automatically. No setup needed — just select Ultra and send.
What is Battle mode?▾
Battle mode sends your message to Claude Sonnet 4 and Claude Opus 4 simultaneously and displays both responses side-by-side. You can vote on which answer is better — it's a great way to understand each model's strengths and decide when to use which.
Are my conversations saved?▾
Yes, automatically. Every conversation is saved to your account in real time. Your full history lives in the sidebar — click any conversation to continue it exactly where you left off. You can also rename, delete, or export any conversation as a Markdown file.
Does it work on mobile?▾
Yes. Apex is fully responsive and works on any modern smartphone or tablet. Voice input is especially useful on mobile — tap the mic icon, speak, and send without typing.
